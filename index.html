Setting

The Multiverse

The universe of this setting utilises The Great Wheel cosmology, where the Prime Material Plane is placed within the Ethereal Plane whitch connects it to the inner planes which are the collective of all the elemental planes, the two energy planes, the Elemental Chaos, and the planes that formed between them. The Prime Material Plane is directly linked to its parallel planes the Fey Wild and The Shodowfell. The Astral Plane connects the Material Plane and all the inner planes to the outer planes. Accessing other planes of existence is common through planar shrines which are powered through wish stones, however other methods of traversing between the planes are possible such as the infamous bag of holding-ception method.

Deities

The deities in this setting are those of the Dawn War Pantheon, The Sovereign Host and The Dark Six from Eberron.

The Campaign

The campaign takes place in a material plane, which is being invaded by abberations that come from many planes, the most notable of which is the Far Realm. The campaign will involve travelling between the different planes to achieve goals and damage the control of the enemies.
