Setting

The Universe

The universe of this setting utilises The Great Wheel cosmology, where the Prime Material Plane is placed within the Ethereal Plane whitch connects it to the inner planes which are the collective of all the elemental planes, the two energy planes, and the planes that formed between them. The Prime Material Plane is directly linked to its parallel planes parallel planes the Fey Wild and The Shodowfell
