Setting

The Multiverse

The universe of this setting utilises The Great Wheel cosmology, where the Prime Material Plane is placed within the Ethereal Plane whitch connects it to the inner planes which are the collective of all the elemental planes, the two energy planes, the Elemental Chaos, and the planes that formed between them. The Prime Material Plane is directly linked to its parallel planes the Fey Wild and The Shodowfell. The Astral Plane connects the Material Plane and all the inner planes to the outer planes.

The Campaign

The campaign takes place in a material plane, which is being invaded by abberations that come from many planes, the most notable of which is the Far Realm.
